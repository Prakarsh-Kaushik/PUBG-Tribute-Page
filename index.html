PUBG : Player's Unknown Battlegrounds - A Tribute

PLAYER's UNKNOWN BATTLEGROUNDS is a last-man-standing shooter being developed with community feedback. Players must fight to locate weapons and supplies in a massive 8x8 km island to be the lone survivor. In the game, up to one hundred players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to kill others while avoiding getting killed themselves. The available safe area of the game's map decreases in size over time, directing surviving players into tighter areas to force encounters. This is Battle Royale.

  • 2017 - PUBG was first released for Microsoft Windows via Steam's early access beta program in March 2017, with a full release in December 2017.
  • 2018 - PUBG Mobile, was released worldwide on March 19, 2018
  • 2019 - One of the Most Played Mobile Games in India
  • 2020 - Banned in India on September 02, 2020
